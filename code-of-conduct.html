MN GAMES - Code of Conduct Welcome to MN Games! Our community thrives on mutual respect, fun, and inclusivity. To maintain a positive environment for all users, we have established the following Code of Conduct. By accessing or using our website, you agree to abide by these guidelines. 1. Respect Others - Treat all users with kindness, courtesy, and respect, regardless of their background, identity, or opinions. - Avoid engaging in any form of harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior. This includes but is not limited to: bullying, hate speech, threats, and personal attacks. - Refrain from posting or sharing any content that may be considered inappropriate, offensive, or harmful to others. 2. Play Fair - Do not cheat, exploit bugs, or use unauthorized tools to gain an unfair advantage in games or competitions. - Respect the rules and regulations of each game and adhere to fair play principles. - Report any cheating or unfair behavior to the website administrators promptly. 3. Protect Privacy - Respect the privacy of others and refrain from sharing personal information without consent. - Do not engage in any form of doxxing, stalking, or invasive behavior. - Be cautious when interacting with others online and avoid sharing sensitive information. 4. Foster a Positive Community - Contribute to a welcoming and friendly atmosphere by being supportive and helpful to fellow users. - Encourage constructive discussions and provide feedback in a constructive manner. - Report any disruptive or harmful behavior to the website moderators. 5. Follow Legal Guidelines - Ensure all content shared or uploaded complies with copyright laws and intellectual property rights. - Do not promote or engage in any illegal activities, including but not limited to piracy, hacking, or distributing malicious software. - Respect the terms of service and usage policies of our website and any third-party services. 6. Parental Guidance - Parents or guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's online activities and guide them on safe internet usage. - Educate children about online safety, including the risks of interacting with strangers and sharing personal information. 7. Enforcement - Violation of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary actions, including warnings, account suspension, or permanent bans. - The website administrators reserve the right to take appropriate measures to maintain a safe and enjoyable environment for all users. 8.
